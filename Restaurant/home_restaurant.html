So - What exactly is "Oasis" anyway? We're glad you asked. The Oasis is a restaurant that seeks to provide its customers with the comfort of being home while maintaining the glamor and ease of going out. Located close to dozens of hiking trails and three state parks, the Oasis is a haven of locally-sourced, organic food and drinks, with award winning chefs working hard to provide you with the best dining experience possible. And with our gorgeous view of the desert, you'll be sure to be entertained by the local wildlife.
